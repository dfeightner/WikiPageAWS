Guide to getting started and navigating this site


Guide

This wiki page will serve as a resource and tool for interns to use while completing their internship. This page will host valuable and useful information that will help guide interns. Please feel free to add any important or useful information not listed on here that will be helpful to future interns.


Navigation

Click on the search bar to look for something specific within these wiki pages. Otherwise, click on the topics in the menu on the left hand side to read more about them. Most pages will have a drop down of other related topics. Please feel free to read through them to learn more. To get back to the main menu, click on the NextGen Logo in the top left corner.
